Innovation clay brooder

Innovation clay brooder heater-usage instructions

  • 1.put the mesh Inside the pot first as it is.
  • 2.put a little red hot charcoal.
  • 3.put a little raw charcoal.
  • 4.leave outside to light,once ready fill the remaining space with raw charcoal upto the neck level.
  • 5.Spread some ash on the top so That smoke comes out through the holes.
  • 6.place the lid on its position,in about 15 min There Will be no more smoke.
  • 7.The brooding pot is safe to go to the brooding room.Please use the shorter side of the stand.
